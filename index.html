About Pubudu Prasangi Jayalath

Passionate Music Educator | Certified Western Music Specialist

With a deep-rooted passion for Western classical music and over 10 years of teaching experience, I am dedicated to nurturing musical talent across the globe. My journey in music education began with my own love for the piano and has evolved into a comprehensive teaching practice that reaches students worldwide.

As a registered teacher with multiple international examination boards, I bring professional expertise and structured learning methodologies to every lesson. My qualifications include a Bachelor's degree in Western Music and certifications from prestigious institutions, ensuring that my students receive world-class music education.


My Teaching Philosophy

I believe that music education should be accessible, enjoyable, and tailored to each student's unique learning style. My approach combines technical excellence with creative expression, helping students not only pass examinations but also develop a lifelong love for music.
